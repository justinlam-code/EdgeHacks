MISSION

Founded by a single goal, EdgeHacks is a student led-non profit focused on creating hackathons to improve coding abilities within the community.

HOW TO JOIN?

To join the EdgeHacks Winter 23/24 Hackathon, participants must initially sign up for the event via Devpost. After completing their registration, they are advised to review the challenge categories and necessary submission guidelines.

TOPIC

Engaging their expertise and creativity, participants should craft innovative solutions poised to foster positive change within their communities and on a larger scale.
